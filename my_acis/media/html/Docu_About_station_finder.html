What is the Station Finder Tool?

Tool to locate weather stations that record daily climate data across the US.

What type of stations/data can be found?

Currently stations belonging to following networks can be found. These stations offer standard climate variables such as Maximum/Minimum/Averager Temperature, Snow, Snowdepth and Degree Days. Temporal resolution is daily.
Networks:

  • WBAN
  • COOP
  • GHCN
  • ICAO
  • NWSLI
  • FAA
  • WMO
  • CoCoRaHS
  • ThreadEx

Summary of Station Networks found in ACIS

What can you do with this tool?

Search for stations by climate element(s) and time range.
Search for stations in a variety of area, e.g. country warning area, county, basin, state, climate division and custom shapes.
Obtain climate summaries for a station (COOP only).
Find available data analysis tools for a station.
