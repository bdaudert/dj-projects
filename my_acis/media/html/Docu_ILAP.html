What is the Western Landscape Explorer?

The Western Landscapes Explorer is a map based tool that provides access to information used to evaluate large landscapes over time. This includes data and information developed by the Integrated Landscape Assessment Project (ILAP), as well as other landscape-level information designed to facilitate decision making, research, and exploration.
ILAP Home Page

What datasets are being used?

Various Geogarphic Information System (GIS) data sets pertaining to vegetation, soil, water sheds and more.
