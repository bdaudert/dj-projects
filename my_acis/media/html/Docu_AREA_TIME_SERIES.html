What is the Spatial Summary Time Series Tool?

An interactive tool to generate time series of basic statistics (max, min, sum, mean)
over custom search areas. Summaries can be generated for eight major climate elements.

  • Maximum Temperature
  • Minimum Temperature
  • Average Temperature
  • Precipitation
  • Degree Days (Heating/Cooling/Growing)

What datasets can be used?

This tool works with the NRCC interpolated, NRCC Hi-Resolution daily gridded datasets developed at the Northern Regional Climate Center (NRCC) as well as with a variety of daily NARCCAP modeled datasets.
