What is the Climate Summary Map Tool?

An interactive tool to generate temporal composite maps for a variety of climate elements. Maps can be generated for individual US states.

What datasets can be used?

This tool works with the NRCC interpolated, NRCC Hi-Resolution daily gridded datasets developed at the Northern Regional Climate Center (NRCC) as well as with a variety of NARCCAP modeled datasets.
