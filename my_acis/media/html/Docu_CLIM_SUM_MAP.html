What is the Temporal Summary Map Tool?

An interactive tool to generate temporal composite maps
for a variety of climate elements.
Following climate elements area available.

  • Maximum Temperature (maxt)
  • Minimum Temperature (mint)
  • Precipitation (pcpn)
  • Degree Days (Heating/Cooling/Growing) (hdd,cdd,gdd)
Maps can be generated for individual US states.

What datasets can be used?

This tool works with the NRCC interpolated and
the NRCC Hi-Resolution daily gridded datasets developed at
the Northern Regional Climate Center (NRCC)
as well as with a variety of NARCCAP modeled datasets.
For more information on NRCC interpolated, NRCC Hi-Resolution:
