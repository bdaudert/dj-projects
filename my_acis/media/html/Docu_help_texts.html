Spatial Resolution: 5 km

NRCC Interpolated High Resolution
Source: Northeast Regional Climate Center (NRCC)
Domain: East of Rockies
Date Range: 2007 - Present
Temporal Resolution: Daily
Spatial Resolution: 5 km
Climate Elements: Maximum/Minimum Temperature, Precipitation, Degree Days

PRISM
Source: PRISM Climate Group
Domain: Continental US
Temporal Resolution:
Daily, Monthly, Yearly
Date Range: 1981- Present, 1895 - Present, 1895 - Present
Spatial Resolution: 4 km
Climate Elements:
Daily: Maximum/Minimum/Average Temperature, Precipitation, Degree Days
Monthly/Yearly: Maximum/Minimum Temperature, Precipitation
Version: Monthly/Yearly “4kmM2”, Daily “4kmD1”
