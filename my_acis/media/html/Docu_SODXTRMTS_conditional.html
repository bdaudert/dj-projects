CONDITIONAL PARAMETERS
Base temperature If you choose Heating, Cooling or Growing Degrees as your climate element, please choose your base temperature. For example, Cooling Degree Days are caluclated as folows: (max temp - min temp)/2 - base temperature
Degree Day Calculation
  • Heating Degree Days base temperature - (max temperature - min temperature)/2
  • Cooling Degree Days (max temperature - min temperature)/2 - base temperature
  • Growing degree Days (max temperature - min temperature)/2 - base temperature

Thresholds for "Number of Days" statistic Fill out this sub section if your monthly statitic is "Number of Days."

  • Less greater or between Count the number of days less than/greater than or between threshold(s).
  • Threshold If you choose less than or greater than above, enter your threshold here.
  • Above/Below If you choose between above, enter your lower(Above) and upper(Below) thresholds here.
