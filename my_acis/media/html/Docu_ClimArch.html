What is the USA Climate Archive?

An interactive web-based tool that can be used to obtain climate data and generate graphs for RAWS (Remote Automated Weather Stations) stations

RAWS Home Page

What dataset is being used?

RAWS station data found in the WRCC (Western Regional Climate Center) data system at subdaily temporal resolutions.

What can you do with this tool?

Obtain daily and monthly climate summaries for all RAW stations

Available elements:

Temperatures
Precipitation
Pressure
Moisture/Dew Point/Humidity
Fuel temperature
Solar Radiation

Download data, plots

Access to metadata

Frequency distributions/histograms

Generate time series

Generate wind roses
