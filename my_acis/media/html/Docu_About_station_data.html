What is this tool?

Access point to Historic Station Data.

What datasets are available?

Temporal Resolution
Daily. We are planning to make sub-daily datasets available in the future.
Observed Quantities
Maximum Temperature(F), Minimum Temperature(F), Observation Time Temperature
Snowfall, Snowdepth
Derived Quantities
Average Temperature(F), Heating/Cooling/Growing Degree Days(F)
Station Networks
Stations in the system belong to one or more of these networks.

  • WBAN
  • COOP
  • GHCN
  • ICAO
  • NWSLI
  • FAA
  • WMO
  • CoCoRaHS
  • ThreadEx

Summary of Station Networks found in ACIS
All data sets are made available through the ACIS system.
ACIS Home Page

What can you do with this tool?

Access and download data
Available Data Formats: .xls, .dat, .txt, html(prints to screen).
Queries can be performed for single or multiple stations at a time.
