What is the Monthly/Seasonal Climate Summaries Tool?

This tool calculates a general climatology for a station based on daily input data. Tables and graphs of monthly and seasonal summaries can be created for the following climate elements.

  • Maximum/Minimum Temperature
  • Precipitation/Snow
  • Heating/Cooling/Growing/Corn Degree Days

What datasets can be used?

This tool works with daily station data. Available networks:

  • COOP
  • GHCN
  • ICAO
  • NWSLI
  • FAA
  • WMO
  • WBAN
  • ThreadEx
  • CoCoRaHS
