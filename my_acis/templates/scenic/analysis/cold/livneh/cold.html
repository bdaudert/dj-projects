Cold Extremes in the Southwest

Intoduction

We idenpngied cold outbreaks by analyzing minimum and maximum temperature data in the linveh dataset by computing a cold index each location (lon, lat coordinate). The cold index at lon, lat coordinate on is defined as the number of degrees below the 5th percentile observed at that location. The percentiles were computed over the winter season (DJF) and year range 1951 - 2005. This analysis is run on the livneh grid, BBOX: -125, 31, -102, 49.1 (103968 locations).
