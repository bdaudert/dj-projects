What this tool?

For a chosen target year time series of daily values is generated.
50% (Climatology), 5%-95%, 10% - 90% and 25% - 75% Percentile ranges are also computed.

What datasets are available?

This tool works with historic station data as well as interpolated gridded datasets.

Temporal Resolution
Daily for station data.
Daily (NRCC-Int, PRISM), monthly/yearly (PRISM)
Observed Quantities
Station/Gridded:
Maximum Temperature (F/C), Minimum Temperature (F/C)
Station only:
Observation Time Temperature (F/C)
Snowfall (in/mm), Snow Depth (in/mm)
Derived Quantities
Station/Gridded:
Heating/Cooling/Growing Degree Days (F/C)
Station only:
Average Temperature (F/C)
Station Networks:
Overview of station networks
