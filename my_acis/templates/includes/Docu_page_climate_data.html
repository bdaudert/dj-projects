What can you find here?

Data Listers
  • Access to data listers for an area of interest
    • Daily Data (all datasets)
    • Monthly/Yearly Data (PRISM only)
Available Data Formats: .xls, .dat, .txt, html(prints to screen).

Data Analysis Tools
  • Spatial Summaries (Time Series over area)
    • Maximum
    • Minimum
    • Mean
    • Sum
  • Temporal Summaries (Maps)
    • Maximum
    • Minimum
    • Mean
    • Sum
Area options
  • county, county warning area
  • climate division, drainage basin
  • custom polygon, circle or bounding box
