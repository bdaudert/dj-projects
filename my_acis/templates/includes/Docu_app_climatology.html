What is this tool?

This tool calculates a general climatology for a station or grid point.
based on daily input data. Creates tables and graphs of monthly and seasonal summaries.

What datasets can be used?

This tool works with historic station data as well as gridded datasets.

Observed Quantities
Station/Gridded:
Maximum Temperature (F/C), Minimum Temperature (F/C)
Station only:
Observation Time Temperature (F/C)
Snowfall (in/mm), Snow Depth (in/mm)
Derived Quantities
Station/Gridded:
Heating/Cooling/Growing Degree Days (F/C)
Station only:
Average Temperature (F/C)
Station Networks:
Overview of station networks
