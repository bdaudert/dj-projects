Climate Engine by Google/DRI and University of Idaho

Visualization of Climate and Remote Sensing Data
through Cloud Computing

Datasets

  • Gridmet
  • MODIS
  • Landsat 5/8
Capabilities
  • Generate custom climate anomaly maps in near-real time
  • Upload custom fusion tables of area of interest
  • Obtain spatially averaged summaries
  • Generate daily, yearly and intra-year time series.
  • Download time series data (cvs) and maps (geotiff)
