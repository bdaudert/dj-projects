What is this tool?

An interactive tool to generate time series of basic statistics
(max, min, sum, mean) over custom search areas.
Summaries can be generated for eight major climate elements.

  • Maximum Temperature
  • Minimum Temperature
  • Average Temperature
  • Precipitation
  • Degree Days (Heating/Cooling/Growing)

What datasets can be used?

Both station and grid data can be used to generate the time series.
