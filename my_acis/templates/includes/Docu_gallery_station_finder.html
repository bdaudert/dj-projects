What is the application?

{{app_names.station_finder}}
You can use this tool to find a particular weather station lying in a
US state, County, County Warning Area, Climate Division or Drainage Basin.
Or you can draw your own custom polygon to find stations or grid points within.
You can find weather stations in an area that satisfy specific conditions
about available variables and record dates.
You can also use this tool to find names and ids of
US Counties, County Warning Areas, Climate Divisions or Drainage Basins
in a US state.

What types of questions can be answered?

  • How many County Warning Areas have area in a particular US state, what are their names?
  • Which weather stations in a particular area record precipitation and temperature data.
  • Which weather stations in a particular area have data records spanning a certain date range.
