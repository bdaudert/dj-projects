What can you find here?

Data and analysis tools for individual weather stations

Data Listers
  • Access to daily data of a weather station
Available Data Formats: .xls, .dat, .txt, html(prints to screen)

Data Analysis Tools
  • Monthly Summaries
    • Monthly Maximum
    • Monthly Minimum
    • Monthly Average
    • Standard Deviation
    • Number of Days (above/below/between thresholds)
    • Range during Month
    • Monthly Sum
  • Yearly Summaries
    • Maximum
    • Minimum
    • Mean
    • Median
    • Sum
  • Inter-year Comparison
  • General Climatology
  • Data Comparison between gridded and station data
