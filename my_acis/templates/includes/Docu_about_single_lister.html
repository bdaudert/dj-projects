What is Station Data Lister tool?

Access point to daily Historic Station Data.

What datasets are available?

Temporal Resolution
Daily. We are planning to make sub-daily datasets available in the future.
For monthly/yearly/seasonal summaries, use ANALYSIS TOOLS
Observed Quantities
Maximum Temperature (F/C), Minimum Temperature (F/C),
Observation Time Temperature (F/C)
Snowfall (in/mm), Snow Depth (in/mm)
Derived Quantities
Average Temperature (F/C), Heating/Cooling/Growing Degree Days (F/C)
Station Networks

  • WBAN
  • COOP
  • GHCN
  • ICAO
  • NWSLI
  • FAA
  • WMO
  • CoCoRaHS
  • ThreadEx
All data sets are made available through the ACIS system.
ACIS Home Page

What can you do with this tool?

Access and download data
Obtain data for

  • Individual station(s)
  • Stations within a county, county warning area
  • Stations within a climate division, drainage basin
  • Stations within a custom polygon, circle or bounding box
Available climate variables
  • Max/Min/Ave Temperatures
  • Precipitation/Snowfall/Snow Depth
  • Heating/Cooling/Growing Degree Days
  • Pan Evaporation/Wind Movement (at select stations)
Available Data Formats: .xls, .dat, .txt, html(prints to screen).
