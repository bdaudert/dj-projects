GeoDataPortal (GDP) by USGS Center for Integrated Data Analysis (CIDA)

Access to Data

  • Downscaled Climate Projections for the US
  • Sea Level Rise Projections
  • Landscape Change Projections
Capabilities
  • Upload shape files to specify area of interest
  • Download large datasets (historic and future climate datasets)
  • Obtain spatially averaged summaries
  • Different output formats (NetCDF, csv)
  • Data Visualization (Climate Indices maps)
  • Dataset comparison
  • Environmental Data Discovery and Transformation Tool (EnDDaT)
