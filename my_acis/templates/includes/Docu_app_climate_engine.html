What is the Climate Engine tool?

A tool to visualize remote sensing and meteorological datasets

What datasets are available?

Remote Sensing Datasets
  • MODIS Terra Remote Sensing
  • Landsat 5 Remote Sensing
  • Landsat 8 Remote Sensing
  • Landsat 4/5/7/8 Remote Sensing
Meteorological Datasets
  • UI METDATA/Gridmet
  • CFS Reanalysis
  • CHRIPS Precipitation
  • UI MACA future climate (CMIP5)
Analysis options
  • Map generation
  • Time Series for points or custom shapes specified by fusion tables
Data summary options
  • Maximum/Minimum
  • Mean/Median
  • Sum
  • Departures from averages
  • Yearly Summaries (time series only)
  • Intra-year comparison (time series only)
