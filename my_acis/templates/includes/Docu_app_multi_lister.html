What is the Data Lister tool?

Access point to historic station and gridded data.

Area options
  • County, County Warning Area
  • Climate Division, Drainage Basin
  • Custom Polygons and Bounding Boxes
Data summary options
  • Maximum/Minimum
  • Mean/Median
  • Sum

What datasets are available?

This tool works with historic station data as well as gridded datasets.
