MODIS (Global)
Source: MODIS archive in Google Earth Engine
Domain: Global
Datasets:
  • Modis Terra (2000-02-24 - Present)
  • Modis Aqua (2000-07-04 - Present)
Temporal Resolution: 8 day
Spatial Resolution: 30m
Climate Variables:
    Vegetation Index (NDVI), Enhanced Vegetation Index (EVI),
    Burn Area Index (BAI),Snow Index (NDSI), Water Index (NDWI),
    Fractional Snow Cover (FSC), Land Surface Temperature (LST)
