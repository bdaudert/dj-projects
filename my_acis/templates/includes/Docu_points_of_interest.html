Region
  • Stations: Search for multiple stations.
      You can enter a comma separated list of station names or IDs.
      Use the Station Finder to find a station names or IDs.
  • Gridpoints: Search for a multiple grid point.
      Enter a comma separated list of Lon,Lat coordinate or
      Use the map interface to find your locations.
          Clicking on the map will add a new marker to the list.
          Clicking on a marker will remove that marker from the list
          Dragging a marker will update the list with the new coordinates.
