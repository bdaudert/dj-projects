Example

Find the highest temperature ever recorded in March at Winnemucca airport, NV.

Step by Step instructions

  1. Go to Single-Point Products/{{app_names.monthly_summary}}



  2. Set the form parameters:

    In the form on the left under "Point of Interest", choose "Station".
    In the "Station ID" field, start typing "Winnemucca".
    Choose "WINNEMUCCA MUNI AP, 269171" from the dropdown menu.



    For "Start Year" and "End Year", type "POR" (Period of Record).

    From the "Variable" drop down menu, select "Maximum Temperature".

    "Statistic Period" is "Monthly".

    For "Statistic", choose "Maximum" because we want to find the highest
    of the maximum temperatures of the month.

    For "Units", select "English".

    For "Max Missing Days", type "0".
    (Months with more than 0 of missing days will not be included in summary statistics)

    For "Departure from Averages", choose "No".

    The completed form looks like this



  3. Submit the request by clicking "Generate Time Series".
  4. Interpreting the results.

    For each year in the period of record of the station,
    the data table shows the maximum of the maximum daily temperatures
    for each month of the year. The last colum shows the maximum annual temperatures.

    There are three ways to find the highest March temperature ever recorded:

    1. You can look at the March column of the data table.


    2. You can look at the summary statistics table.


    3. You can look at the graph.
