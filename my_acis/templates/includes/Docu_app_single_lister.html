What is the Data Lister tool?

Access to historic station and grid point data.

What datasets are available?

This tool works with historic station data as well as gridded datasets.

Temporal Resolution
Daily for station data.
Daily (NARRCAP, PRISM), monthly/yearly (PRISM)
Observed Quantities
Station/Gridded:
Maximum Temperature (F/C), Minimum Temperature (F/C)
Station only:
Observation Time Temperature (F/C)
Snowfall (in/mm), Snow Depth (in/mm)
Derived Quantities
Station/Gridded:
Heating/Cooling/Growing Degree Days (F/C)
Station only:
Average Temperature (F/C)
