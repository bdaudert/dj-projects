What is this tool?

This tool calculates a general climatology for a station
based on daily input data. Tables and graphs of monthly and seasonal summaries
can be created for the following climate elements.

  • Maximum/Minimum Temperature
  • Precipitation/Snow
  • Heating/Cooling/Growing/Corn Degree Days

What datasets can be used?

This tool works with daily station data from several Networks
