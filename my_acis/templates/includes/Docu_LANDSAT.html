LANDSAT (Global)
Source: LANDSAT archive in Google Earth Engine
Domain: Global
Datasets:
  • Landsat Top of Atmosphere (TOA)
    • L5_TOA (1984-04-13 to 2011-11-17)
    • L7_TOA (1999-05-28 - Present)
    • L8_TOA (2013-04-07 - Present)
    • L_TOA (L4/5/7/8) (1982-01-01 - Present)
  • Landsat Surface Reflectance (SR)
    • L5_SR (1984-04-13 to 2011-11-17)
    • L7_SR (1999-05-28 - Present)
    • L8_SR (2013-04-07 - Present)
    • L_SR (L4/5/7/8) (1982-01-01 - Present)
Temporal Resolution: 8 day
Spatial Resolution: 30m
Climate Variables:
    Vegetation Index (NDVI), Enhanced Vegetation Index (EVI),
    Burn Area Index (BAI),Snow Index (NDSI), Water Index (NDWI),
    Fractional Snow Cover (FSC), Ocean Clorophyll (OC2)
    True Color (Red/Green/Blue), False Color (NIR/Red/Green)
